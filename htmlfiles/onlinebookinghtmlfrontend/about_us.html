A perfect choice for your stay

Business or leisure travellers will find Ritz Garden Hotel a perfect choice as we are strategically located in the heart of Ipoh city! Centralised between Old and New Town, we are easily accessible to most commercial addresses and tourist spots. Yet, the old charm of Ipoh beckons from the quaint Old Town where one can still find their favourite eateries and landmark Heritage buildings.

Ritz Garden Hotel and You

Our new Garden Wing is designed to integrate the old and the new. This building is an integral part of Jalan Yang Kalsom / Jalan Hussein history. Our hotel owners have painstakingly preserved the old yet honouring the past while bringing modern days convenience to mind.

After exploring Ipoh Old Town and satiated by our equally famous hawker food, it is time for a lazy and languid afternoon, our cool and comfortable swimming pool awaits......

To ensure that you stay connected with your friends and business partners, our newly upgraded WIFI connection enable you hassle free convenience from all our rooms and hotel area.
